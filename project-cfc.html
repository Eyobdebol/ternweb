CFC (Asela Silo Project)

Silo Installation

Project Description

Assembly and installation of grain silos and towers with underground tunnel conveyors for CFC in Asela, featuring:

  • 5 silos with 2,500 ton total capacity
  • 150m of underground conveyor tunnels
  • Automated filling and emptying systems
  • Dust control systems

Technical Innovation

Implemented a first-of-its-kind underground conveyor system in Ethiopia to maximize space efficiency.

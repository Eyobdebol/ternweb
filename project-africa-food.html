Africa Food Complex Adama

Silo Installation

Project Details

TERN Engineering executed the complete civil works and preparation for silo foundations at Africa Food Complex in Adama, including:

  • Site preparation and leveling
  • Reinforced concrete foundation construction
  • Utility connections preparation
  • Quality control testing

Project Highlights

The project was completed 2 weeks ahead of schedule, with foundations capable of supporting 10,000 tons of grain storage capacity.

Afar Bentonite Manufacturing Plant

Industrial Plant

Project Overview

TERN Engineering designed and installed a complete bentonite manufacturing plant in the Afar region, capable of processing 50 tons of raw material per day. The project included:

  • Custom milling and processing equipment
  • Dust collection systems
  • Material handling conveyors
  • Quality control laboratory setup

Key Achievements

Completed within 6 months despite challenging environmental conditions, the plant now produces high-quality bentonite products for industrial applications across Ethiopia.

About TERN

Our journey and commitment to engineering excellence

Pioneering Mechanical Engineering Solutions

Established in 2000 by founder Tesfaye Debol, TERN Mechanical Engineering has been at the forefront of Ethiopia's manufacturing and engineering services sector, consistently pursuing technological advancement and innovation.

Our team consists of expert professionals operating with one primary objective: customer satisfaction through customization, flexibility, and adaptability to meet diverse project requirements.
